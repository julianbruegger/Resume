# VitaFlow Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Everyone who needs a resume — students, career-changers, mid-level professionals, executives. The defining trait is low effort: users want a polished result without becoming a design expert. They're likely stressed about job hunting and want the tool to feel effortless and trustworthy.

### Brand Personality
**Clean. Modern. Effortless.**

Emotional goals: confidence (the output looks professional), calm (the interface doesn't overwhelm), and a subtle delight that makes it feel like a premium product worth using.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Refined minimalism with purposeful depth — think Reactive Resume (rxresu.me) as the primary reference. Clean surfaces, generous whitespace, crisp typography, subtle but intentional motion.
- **Theme**: Full light + dark mode support. Dark mode should feel elegant, not "developer tool" — deep neutrals, not pure black; soft contrast, not neon-on-black.
- **Anti-references**: Avoid heavy gradients, glassmorphism for decoration, icon-above-every-heading templates, AI color palettes (cyan-on-dark, purple-to-blue gradients). Should NOT look like a generic SaaS template.
- **Animations**: Smooth, purposeful transitions that reinforce quality. Ease-out deceleration, staggered reveals on load, micro-interactions on hover/focus. Nothing bouncy or elastic.
- **Typography**: Should use a real display/body font pairing — not system fonts. Something refined but approachable.

### Design Principles
1. **Effortless clarity** — Every screen should be immediately understandable. No hunting for actions, no unclear states.
2. **Premium through restraint** — Quality comes from spacing, typography, and motion — not decoration. Remove rather than add.
3. **Dark mode is first-class** — Not an afterthought. Both themes should look intentionally designed.
4. **Motion with purpose** — Every animation should communicate something (state change, hierarchy, completion). No animation for its own sake.
5. **Trust through polish** — Job seekers are anxious. A meticulously finished interface signals that their resume will be equally polished.
